About Startup Domain Search

A free tool for founders and indie hackers. Generate 60 AI-powered domain candidates, check availability, and score every name to pick the best one with confidence.

✍️
1 · Generate

Describe your startup idea. AI generates 60 creative, brandable domain name candidates tailored to your concept.

🌐
2 · Check

Real-time availability checked across the TLDs you care about — .com, .io, .ai, .app, .dev and more — via RDAP, no signup needed.

🏆
3 · Score & Choose

Star names you like. Each gets scored on 6 dimensions. Adjust weights. The TOTAL column ranks your shortlist — pick the winner.

Score dimensions

LEN · shorter = better PRO · easy to say aloud MEM · sticks in memory BRD · invented & unique ZON · available TLDs FIT · AI relevance to your idea
⚡
No setup needed. Bundled Groq key works out of the box. Paste your own Groq, OpenAI, or Anthropic key in the AI provider field for heavy use.
🔒
100% private. Everything stays in your browser's localStorage. No account, no data collected, no server.
